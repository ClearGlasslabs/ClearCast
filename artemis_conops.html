ClearGlassInc Artemis: CONOPS

This HTML view keeps the CONOPS readable online and offline. For source-controlled markdown, open palantir_artemis_conops.md.

Platform Stack

  • Gotham: Operational intelligence and casework.
  • Foundry: Data integration, ontology, and application logic.
  • AIP: AI copilots, agents, evaluations, and workflow automation.
  • Apollo: Secure deployment, rollout control, and rollback.

Mission Workflow

  1. Ingest live and historical events into ontology-backed mission context.
  2. Run triage, enrichment, and correlation agents with policy checks.
  3. Propose recommendations with explicit confidence and provenance.
  4. Require human approval for operationally significant actions.
  5. Capture outcomes and feed eval pipelines for safe workflow upgrades.

Self-Improvement Guardrails

  • No autonomous goal changes.
  • Versioned prompts and workflow definitions with rollback.
  • Human-in-the-loop approval before any policy/model promotion.
  • Immutable audit trail for every recommendation and override.
