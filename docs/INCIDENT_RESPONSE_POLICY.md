# Incident Response Policy

## Objective
Ensure rapid, auditable, and coordinated response to cybersecurity incidents.

## Response Lifecycle
1. Detection and triage.
2. Containment and evidence preservation.
3. Eradication and recovery.
4. Post-incident review and control updates.

## SLAs
- Critical incidents: immediate triage and executive notification.
- High incidents: triage within 1 hour.
- Medium incidents: triage within 4 hours.

## Governance
Every incident receives a case record, timeline, owner, and post-incident action plan.
